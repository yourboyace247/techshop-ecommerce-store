TechShop e-commerce Store:

1.Home Page: hero-content describing the e-shop, and a slide show showcasing the products.
2.Products Page: Iteams loading from the JSON file. You can filter them by category (Laptops, Smartphones etc...), sort them (price low-high, high-low, & by name a-z), and you can add them in your cart.
3.Cart Page: Cart is showing you the products you have added in it. You can deduct an item, add more, or delete it. Order summary has the price of your products, shipping cost, & taxes.
4.Shipping Page: You can proceed to this page only if you have items in your cart. In this page you add your shipping details (Full Name, Address, Zip code, & Country), & the order summary details from the cart page.
5.Payment page: You can proceed to this page only if you add your shipping details. In this page you add your credit cart details (Number, CVV, Expiry date, & Name on the card), & the order summary details from the cart page.
6.Thank you page: You can proceed to this page only if you add your card details info. Page display a message thanking you for your order, and has some contact info of the store in case you want to reach out.
